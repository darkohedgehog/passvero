# Project Context

## Product name

**Passvero**

## Product category

Multilingual SaaS platform for Digital Product Passports.

## Primary users

- small and medium-sized manufacturers;
- importers;
- distributors;
- private-label brands;
- product compliance teams;
- agencies that prepare product documentation for clients.

## Core problem

Many smaller companies do not have a structured system for publishing product origin, materials, technical information, repairability, spare-parts information, certificates and recycling instructions.

Passvero should make this process simple:

1. create a company account;
2. add a product;
3. enter structured product data;
4. upload supporting documents;
5. publish a digital passport;
6. generate a QR code;
7. place the QR code on the product or packaging;
8. allow customers and partners to scan and view the public passport.

## Product positioning

Passvero is a **DPP-ready product information platform**.

Until category-specific regulatory requirements are fully implemented and legally reviewed, avoid marketing claims such as:

- “100% EU compliant”;
- “guaranteed legal compliance”;
- “official EU certification”.

Preferred wording:

- “Built for Digital Product Passport workflows”;
- “Prepare and publish structured product information”;
- “DPP-ready product data management”;
- “Support your product transparency and compliance processes”.

## MVP capabilities

The MVP should include:

- multilingual marketing website;
- user authentication;
- organization onboarding;
- organization members and roles;
- product CRUD;
- product publication status;
- versioned passport data;
- materials and composition;
- manufacturer and origin data;
- repair and spare-parts information;
- recycling and disposal information;
- document uploads;
- public passport pages;
- QR code generation;
- basic scan analytics;
- plan limits;
- billing later in the MVP sequence.

## Out of scope for the first implementation phase

- blockchain;
- NFC hardware provisioning;
- GS1 EPCIS implementation;
- ERP integrations;
- WooCommerce or Shopify sync;
- AI-generated compliance claims;
- automated legal certification;
- enterprise SSO;
- advanced analytics;
- mobile native apps.

## Product principles

- simple for non-technical users;
- structured but not overwhelming;
- mobile-friendly public passport pages;
- secure organization isolation;
- multilingual from the beginning;
- accessible and fast;
- honest about regulatory scope.
